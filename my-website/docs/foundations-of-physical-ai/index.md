# Foundations of Physical AI

Introduction to Physical AI concepts and principles

## Overview
This module introduces the fundamental concepts of Physical AI, which combines artificial intelligence with physical systems. You'll learn about the theoretical foundations that underpin humanoid robotics and how AI algorithms interact with real-world physics.
